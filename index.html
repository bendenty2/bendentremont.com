Ben's Place

about

I'm Ben! I take photos as a hobby, almost entirely with my long lens. Most of what you'll find here is wildlife and nature, plus the occasional landscape.

My gear is humble, but I've made peace with that. What matters more to me is being out there, being patient, and observing the world. This small personal archive showcases some of my favourites — wildlife I've watched, and places I want to remember.
